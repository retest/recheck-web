  • Has traditional GUI test automation failed you? Create robust tests with less effort — easily maintained.




    Have you had enough
    of traditional GUI test automation    ?
    We present you the first of its kind GUI regression testing tool. No need to manually create assertions during test automation. Using redundant information during component recognition creates robust tests. Which can be maintained by a single click. But best of all:
    Use an artificially intelligent monkey to fully automatically test your application.

  • Less effort.

    No need to create assertions:
    A small table with five rows and columns would easily require you to specify 25 assertions for a single test. With retest you go the other way around: Record the complete GUI state during capturing and easily compare it during replay. Differences are either improvements which can be accepted with a single click, volatile elements which can be permanently ignored, and regressions - which is the purpose of the test in the first place. No need to record any assertions at all.

  • Robust tests.

    With traditional GUI test automation, you have to decide with which attribute you want to identify a component. If your chosen attribute changes, your test fails although the application still works. With retest you don't need to decide for an attribute ... you simply use them all. This redundancy would normally mean a lot of additional effort. But not with retest. Maintaining your test happens with a single click.
    retest compares the whole picture instead of a single piece of the puzzle.


  • Intelligent monkey testing.




    The future of testing is in AI.
    retest stems from a research project and brings that intelligence to you. Have our monkey test your application 24/7 for crashes. Have him optimize the tests for coverage by analyzing your bytecode. Train him by recording use cases that he can vary. Use blacklisting and whitelisting to have him concentrate on areas that you want to be scrutinized. And rerun the tests to see how the behavior of your application changes.
    The future of testing is now.

Recording tests by users.

Modern test capturing tools are so complex, professional testers need weeks of training. Recording realistic test cases with business users? Impossible.
But not with retest. Without the need to technically specify targets or even record assertions, test creation becomes so easy a monkey could do it.

No more brittle tests.

Our innovative new approach to GUI testing (patent pending) creates tests that are way more robust than anything you have ever seen before. If our tests fail, they do because your application is broken, not because the test is broken.

Maintenance with a single click.

The more tests you have, the more test maintenance becomes a burden. Not with retest. Our innovative approach to maintain tests with a single click (patent pending) is groundbreaking. Suddenly, reviewing test results and maintaining differences is fun. Your testers are going to love it.
